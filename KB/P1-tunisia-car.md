### Two Regime Options

- **Plates:** RS series
- **Restriction:** Cannot sell - EVER (unless regularized)
- **Who can drive:** Owner, spouse, or others WITH owner present

- **Tax:** 25% or 30% of normal duties

- **Plates:** Regular TN series
- **Restriction:** Cannot sell for 5 years
- **Who can drive:** Anyone (normal car)

### Currency Requirement (NEW 2024)

- Must import foreign currency equal to tax value
- OR debit from existing devises account in Tunisia
- Must be done within 1 year before import

### Timeline

- 24 months from "derni√®re entr√©e" to complete import/purchase

### Eligibility Criteria

| Criterion         | Requirement                                                  |

| Family definition | Married couple + minor children, OR single head of household |
| Income ceiling    | ‚â§10√ó SMIG (individual) or ‚â§14√ó SMIG (couple)                 |

| Electric/Hybrid             | 0%              | 7%  |

- Cannot combine with FCR or "Voiture Populaire"

## 4. Tax Calculations

### Standard Import Duties (Without FCR)

```
Base: CIF Value (Car + Shipping + Insurance)

Duties typically include:
- Customs duty: 10-43% (varies by origin)
- Consumption tax: 10-88% (based on engine)
- TVA: 19%
- Other fees: stamps, homologation

Total can reach: 150-250% of car value
```

### FCR Renouvelable Calculation

```
Franchise Totale (RS): 0 TND
Franchise Partielle: 
  - 25% √ó normal_duties (small engine)
  - 30% √ó normal_duties (large engine)
```

### "Une Voiture" Calculation

