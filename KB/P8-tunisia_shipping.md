## üì¶ Option 3: Container Shipping

### When to Use Container

- Non-running vehicle
- High-value/classic car (better protection)
- Want to ship personal belongings with car
- Multiple vehicles

### Container Options

| Type                       | Description           | Cost (Europe ‚Üí Tunisia) |
|----------------------------|-----------------------|-------------------------|
| **Shared container (LCL)** | Car shares space      | ‚Ç¨800 - ‚Ç¨1,500           |

| **40ft exclusive**         | 2 cars or car + goods | ‚Ç¨2,200 - ‚Ç¨3,500         |

### Container Advantages

- ‚úÖ Protected from weather
- ‚úÖ Can include spare parts, personal items
- ‚úÖ Non-running vehicles OK
- ‚úÖ Less handling damage risk

### Container Disadvantages

- ‚ùå More expensive
- ‚ùå Longer transit (waiting for consolidation)
- ‚ùå More paperwork

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warnings

### Age Restrictions

- **FCR vehicles**: Max 5 years old at import
- **USA imports**: Some sources say 5 years max, verify with customs
- **"Une Voiture" program**: Max 8 years old

### Condition Requirements

- RoRo requires **running vehicle**
- Salvage/accident vehicles need **container**
- Check Tunisian homologation requirements

### Timing Considerations

- **Summer (June-Sept)**: Higher prices, book early
- **Ramadan period**: Some delays possible
- **Port strikes**: Monitor news before shipping

### Insurance

- Always get **marine insurance** (typically 1-2% of car value)
- Check if your policy covers **door-to-door** or just **port-to-port**
