# Voiture Populaire (Subsidized Cars Program) - Guide 2026

> **Last Updated:** January 2026
> **Applies to:** Low-income Tunisian residents
> **Program Manager:** Ministère du Commerce

---

## Overview

Government program providing subsidized new cars for low-income citizens.

| Aspect          | Details                   |
|-----------------|---------------------------|
| Annual quota    | ~10,000 units (2025)      |
| Price range     | 29,000 - 35,000 TND       |
| Vehicle type    | New cars only             |
| Frequency       | Every 7 years             |

---

## Eligibility Criteria

| Criterion        | Requirement                                     |
|------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Nationality      | Tunisian                                        |
| Income (single)  | ≤10× SMIG (~4,593 TND/month)                    |
| Income (couple)  | ≤15× SMIG (~6,889 TND/month)                    |
| Previous benefit | Not in last 7 years                             |
| Current vehicle  | Cannot have owned one in last 5 years           |
| Excluded         | Taxi drivers, driving schools, rental companies |

---

## Vehicle Specifications

| Requirement | Value                 |
|-------------|-----------------------|
| Power       | 4 CV fiscaux maximum  |
| Engine      | <1,200 cc (1.2L)      |
| Condition   | New only              |

## Application Process

1. Register at approved concessionnaire
2. Submit documents (ID, income proof, birth certificate)
3. Wait for quota allocation
4. Pay and receive car

---

## Required Documents

- CIN (Carte d'Identité Nationale)
- Proof of income / CNSS attestation
- Birth certificate (extrait de naissance)
- Proof of residence
- Declaration of no current vehicle ownership

---

## Restrictions

- Cannot combine with FCR or "Une Voiture pour Chaque Famille"
- Long waiting lists (varies by model - see table above)
- Cannot resell for a specified period
