## Two Regime Options

### Option A: Franchise Totale (RS Plates)

- **Tax:** 0% (only stamp fees)
- **Plates:** RS series
- **Restriction:** Cannot sell - EVER (unless regularized)
- **Who can drive:** Owner, spouse, or others WITH owner present
- **Authorization needed for:** Parents, children, siblings

### Option B: Franchise Partielle (TN Plates)

- **Tax:** 25% or 30% of normal duties
    - 25% → Essence ≤2000cc, Diesel ≤2500cc, motos
    - 30% → Larger engines
- **Plates:** Regular TN series
- **Restriction:** Cannot sell for 5 years
- **Who can drive:** Anyone (normal car)

---

## Currency Requirement (NEW 2024)

- Must import foreign currency equal to tax value
- OR debit from existing devises account in Tunisia
- Must be done within 1 year before import

### Financial Documents

---

## FCR Import Procedure

### 1. BEFORE LEAVING HOST COUNTRY

- Buy car, get all documents
- Arrange shipping (RoRo or container)
- Import devises to Tunisia

### 2. AT TUNISIAN CUSTOMS (Port/Airport)

- File déclaration en douane
- Choose RS or TN regime
- Pay applicable duties (if partial)
- Get temporary circulation permit

### 3. POST-IMPORT (within 3 months)

- Homologation at ATT
- Insurance
- Final registration (carte grise)

## RS Regularization

After 2 years with RS plates, you may regularize to TN plates by paying 30% of the remaining duties. This allows normal sale and usage.
