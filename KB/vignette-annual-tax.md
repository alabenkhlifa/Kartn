## 4. Payment Deadlines

### 2026 Calendar

| Deadline            | Who Must Pay                                               |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| **10 January 2026** | Transport vehicles (taxis, louages, semi-trailers) - TUCTR |
| **5 February 2026** | Companies, public institutions, state vehicles             |
| **5 March 2026**    | Individuals - **EVEN** plate numbers (pair)                |
| **5 April 2026**    | Individuals - **ODD** plate numbers (impair)               |
| **5 May 2026**      | Rental vehicles & leasing (Ij√¢ra) contracts                |

### How to Determine Pair/Impair

Look at the **LAST digit** of your registration number:

| Last Digit    | Type             | Deadline |
|---------------|------------------|----------|
| 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 | **PAIR** (Even)  | 5 March  |
| 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 | **IMPAIR** (Odd) | 5 April  |

**Example:**

- `123 TU 456` ‚Üí Last digit is **6** ‚Üí PAIR ‚Üí 5 March
- `789 TU 123` ‚Üí Last digit is **3** ‚Üí IMPAIR ‚Üí 5 April

## 6. EV & Hybrid Benefits

### 100% Electric Vehicles (BEV) ‚ö°

| Benefit                | Details                   |
|------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Vignette Reduction** | **50% off** standard rate |
| **Calculation**        | Based on equivalent CV    |

**Example:**

- A 7 CV electric vehicle (if equivalent):
- Normal Essence rate: 130 TND
- EV rate: **65 TND** (50% off)

### Hybrid Vehicles (HEV/PHEV) üîã

| Type                       | Vignette Treatment               |
|----------------------------|----------------------------------|
| **Non-rechargeable (HEV)** | NO reduction - pays thermal rate |
| **Plug-in (PHEV)**         | NO specific vignette reduction   |

**‚ö†Ô∏è Important:** Unlike purchase taxes, hybrid vehicles get **NO vignette discount**

The vignette is calculated based on:

- The thermal engine's fuel type (Essence or Diesel)
- The vehicle's puissance fiscale

**Combined with 2026 Purchase Incentives:**

- PHEV: 0% consumption tax + 7% TVA + **full vignette**
- BEV: 0% consumption tax + 7% TVA + **50% vignette**

This makes **BEV superior** for long-term ownership costs

## 7. Penalties & Amnesty

### Late Payment Penalties

| Delay          | Penalty                     |
|----------------|-----------------------------|
| **< 6 months** | **50%** of vignette amount  |
| **> 6 months** | **100%** of vignette amount |

**Example:**

- 7 CV Essence vignette: 130 TND
- 3 months late: 130 + 65 (50%) = **195 TND**
- 8 months late: 130 + 130 (100%) = **260 TND**

### 2026 Amnesty üéÅ

**Exceptional measure in Loi de Finances 2026:**

> If you pay the 2026 vignette **within the legal deadlines**, all unpaid vignettes from years **before 2025** are *
*forgiven FREE of charge** - no penalties, no back payments!

**Conditions:**

- Pay 2026 vignette before your deadline
- Applies to arrears from 2024 and earlier
- Does NOT apply to 2025 arrears

**This is a one-time opportunity** to clear old vignette debt!

### Other Administrative Consequences

If vignette is unpaid:

- ‚ùå Cannot renew insurance
- ‚ùå Cannot complete vehicle sale (mutation)
- ‚ùå Cannot pass technical inspection
- ‚ùå Risk of fines at police checkpoints

```python
def calculate_vignette(cv, fuel_type, is_company, is_ev):
    # Base rates for individuals
    rates = {
        "essence": {4: 65, 7: 130, 8: 180, 9: 180, 11: 230, 13: 1050, 15: 1400, 99: 2100},
        "diesel": {4: 215, 7: 280, 8: 330, 9: 405, 11: 455, 13: 1275, 15: 1625, 99: 2325},
        "gpl": {4: 390, 7: 455, 8: 505, 9: 580, 11: 630, 13: 1450, 15: 1800, 99: 2500}
    }

    # Find applicable CV bracket
    brackets = [4, 7, 8, 9, 11, 13, 15, 99]
    bracket = next(b for b in brackets if cv <= b)

    # Get base rate
    base = rates[fuel_type][bracket]

    # Apply company multiplier
    if is_company:
        base *= 2

    # Apply EV discount
    if is_ev:
        base *= 0.5

    return base
```
