# Une Voiture pour Chaque Famille (Residents) - Guide 2026

> **Last Updated:** January 2026
> **Applies to:** Tunisian Residents (non-TRE)
> **Status:** ⚠️ AWAITING décrets d'application (expected within 6 months)

---

## Legal Basis

- **Loi de Finances 2026**, Article 55
- Adopted: November 29, 2025 (131 votes)

---

## Eligibility Criteria

| Criterion         | Requirement                                                  |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Residency         | Must live IN Tunisia                                         |
| Frequency         | ONE TIME per family (lifetime)                               |
| Family definition | Married couple + minor children, OR single head of household |
| Income ceiling    | ≤10× SMIG (individual) or ≤14× SMIG (couple)                 |
| Current vehicle   | Must NOT own a car <8 years old                              |

---

## Vehicle Requirements

| Criterion       | Requirement                |
|-----------------|----------------------------|
| Age             | ≤8 years old               |
| Essence engine  | ≤1400 cc (1.4L)            |
| Diesel engine   | ≤1700 cc (1.7L)            |
| Electric/Hybrid | Allowed (bonus exemptions) |

---

## Tax Rates (Reduced)

| Vehicle Type                | Consumption Tax | TVA |
|-----------------------------|-----------------|-----|
| Standard (within cc limits) | 10%             | 7%  |
| Electric/Hybrid             | 0%              | 7%  |
| Locally assembled           | 0%              | 7%  |

*Compare to normal: ~150-200% total duties*

---

## Financing Options

1. **TRE family gifts (dons)** - Relatives abroad can fund
2. **Allocation touristique** - ~12,000 TND per couple
3. **BCT authorization** - Request additional forex if needed

---

## Restrictions

- Cannot combine with FCR or "Voiture Populaire"
- Annual quota: 10% of concessionnaire imports
- Quota system means waiting lists likely
- Cannot sell vehicle for 5 years

## Key Differences: Une Voiture vs FCR vs Voiture Populaire

| Aspect           | Une Voiture Famille | FCR Renouvelable | Voiture Populaire |
|------------------|---------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Who              | Residents           | TRE (expats)     | Low-income        |
| Frequency        | Once/lifetime       | Every 10 years   | Every 7 years     |
| Vehicle Age      | ≤8 years            | ≤5 years         | New only          |
| Tax Benefit      | ~17% total          | 0% or 25-30%     | Subsidized        |
| Can Combine?     | No                  | No               | No                |
