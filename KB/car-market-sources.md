## Car Buying Options Summary

| Option                  | Who          | Frequency      | Car Age  | Tax Benefit                   |
|-------------------------|--------------|----------------|----------|-------------------------------|
| **FCR Renouvelable**    | TRE (expats) | Every 10 years | ≤5 years | 0% or 25-30%                  |
| **Une Voiture/Famille** | Residents    | Once/lifetime  | ≤8 years | ~17% total                    |
| **Voiture Populaire**   | Low-income   | Every 7 years  | New only | Subsidized                    |
| **Regular Market**      | Anyone       | Anytime        | Any      | Full price (~150-200% duties) |

## Chatbot Decision Tree

```
"How do you want to buy a car?"
    │
    ├─→ "Do you live abroad (TRE)?"
    │      ├─ YES (≥2 years) → FCR Renouvelable
    │      │     └─ Compare: Import vs Buy local with FCR
    │      │
    │      └─ NO → "What's your monthly income?"
    │            │
    │            ├─ Low (≤10× SMIG) → Voiture Populaire OR Une Voiture
    │            │     └─ Compare wait times & models
    │            │
    │            ├─ Medium → "Une Voiture pour Chaque Famille"
    │            │     └─ If eligible (no car <8y, income OK)
    │            │
    │            └─ High (>14× SMIG) → Regular Market
    │                  └─ Show automobile.tn options
```
