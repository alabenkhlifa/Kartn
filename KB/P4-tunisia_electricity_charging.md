| Driving Pattern | Monthly km | kWh Needed | Impact on Bill              |
|-----------------|------------|------------|-----------------------------|
| Light use       | 500 km     | ~100 kWh   | Minimal - stays in low tier |

| Heavy use       | 1,500 km   | ~265 kWh   | Likely higher tier          |
| Very heavy      | 2,000 km   | ~350 kWh   | Definitely highest tier     |

### ‚ö° The Tier Jump Problem

If your household already consumes ~150 kWh/month:

- **Before EV:** 150 kWh √ó 12 = 1,800 kWh/year ‚Üí **181 mill/kWh**
- **After EV (+175 kWh):** 325 kWh √ó 12 = 3,900 kWh/year ‚Üí **338 mill/kWh**

**This tier jump nearly DOUBLES your electricity rate!**

## üì± Chatbot Logic: Energy Cost Calculator

### Key Questions to Ask Users

1. **"What's your current monthly electricity consumption?"**
    - Low (<150 kWh) ‚Üí EV won't push you to higher tier
    - Medium (150-250 kWh) ‚Üí Warning about tier jump
    - High (>300 kWh) ‚Üí Already in high tier, EV impact minimal

2. **"How many km do you drive monthly?"**
    - Calculate additional kWh needed
    - Project tier impact

3. **"Do you have solar panels or plan to install?"**
    - Yes ‚Üí Running costs near zero
    - No ‚Üí Factor in tier jump risk

4. **"Can you charge during off-peak hours?"**
    - Night charging = lower demand on grid
    - Some future programs may offer off-peak rates

### Cost Calculation Formula

```python
def calculate_ev_running_cost(monthly_km, current_consumption_kwh):
    # EV consumption: ~17 kWh/100km average
    ev_kwh_needed = (monthly_km / 100) * 17

    total_consumption = current_consumption_kwh + ev_kwh_needed
    annual_consumption = total_consumption * 12

    # Determine tier
    if annual_consumption <= 2400:
        rate = 0.205  # TND/kWh with taxes
    elif annual_consumption <= 3600:
        rate = 0.252
    elif annual_consumption <= 6000:
        rate = 0.382
    else:
        rate = 0.473

    monthly_ev_cost = ev_kwh_needed * rate
    annual_ev_cost = monthly_ev_cost * 12

    # Compare to petrol (7L/100km √ó 2.525 TND)
    petrol_cost = (monthly_km / 100) * 7 * 2.525 * 12

    annual_savings = petrol_cost - annual_ev_cost

    return {
        'monthly_ev_cost': monthly_ev_cost,
        'annual_ev_cost': annual_ev_cost,
        'annual_petrol_cost': petrol_cost,
        'annual_savings': annual_savings,
        'electricity_tier': rate
    }
```
