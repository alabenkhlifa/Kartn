### Price Factors

| Factor               | Impact     | Notes                                       |
|----------------------|------------|---------------------------------------------|
| **Age of Driver**    | High       | 50s = lowest (768 TND avg), young = highest |
| **Gender**           | Medium     | Men pay ~114 TND more (878 vs 764 TND)      |
| **Vehicle Age**      | High       | Newer = higher premiums                     |
| **Vehicle Value**    | High       | Base for Tous Risques calculation           |
| **Engine Size (CV)** | Medium     | Higher CV = higher premium                  |
| **Vehicle Type**     | High       | 4x4/SUV typically higher                    |
| **Bonus-Malus**      | High       | Good history = discounts                    |
| **Annual Mileage**   | Low-Medium | Lower km = potential discount               |
| **Geographic Area**  | Low        | Urban slightly higher                       |
| **Payment Method**   | Low        | Annual lump sum cheaper than monthly        |

### Bonus-Malus System

- Managed centrally (Centrale des Risques)
- Good drivers earn bonus (discount)
- Claims increase malus (surcharge)
- Follows driver, not vehicle
- All insurers must apply same scale

### Ways to Reduce Premiums

1. ‚úÖ Pay annually (avoid monthly interest)
2. ‚úÖ Add low-risk second driver
3. ‚úÖ Take advanced driving course
4. ‚úÖ Consider telematics ("black box") policies
5. ‚úÖ Compare quotes (use assurance.tn comparator)
6. ‚úÖ Don't auto-renew blindly
7. ‚úÖ Choose higher deductible (franchise)
8. ‚ùå Don't add young/new drivers
9. ‚ùå Don't underestimate mileage (voids claims)

## 5. Special Cases: FCR & Imported Cars

### RS Plate Vehicles (Franchise Totale)

| Aspect                       | Requirement                                    |
|------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| **Can be insured?**          | ‚úÖ Yes, normal insurance                        |
| **Driver restrictions**      | Owner, spouse, or others WITH owner present    |
| **Authorization needed**     | Parents, children, siblings need douane permit |
| **Selling**                  | Not allowed (incessible)                       |
| **Insurance considerations** | Same pricing as TN plates                      |

### TN Plate FCR Vehicles (Franchise Partielle)

| Aspect                 | Requirement                         |
|------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| **Can be insured?**    | ‚úÖ Yes, completely normal            |
| **Resale restriction** | 2 years (updated 2025, was 5 years) |
| **Insurance**          | Standard policies apply             |
| **Considerations**     | Value may be higher than local cars |

### Insurance for Imported Cars - Key Considerations

1. **Vehicle Value Declaration**
    - Declare accurate import value (CIF)
    - Under-declaration = claim problems
    - Over-declaration = overpaying premium

2. **Spare Parts Availability**
    - Insurers may consider this
    - Common European brands = standard rates
    - Rare models = potentially higher or limited coverage

3. **Homologation**
    - Must have ATT certification
    - Required for insurance validity

4. **First-Time Registration**
    - Need valid customs clearance
    - Temporary circulation permit acceptable initially

## 8. Claims Process

### Immediate Steps After Accident

1. **Ensure safety** of all parties
2. **Call police/secours** if needed
3. **Take photos** of:
    - Vehicle damage
    - Accident scene
    - Other vehicle's insurance attestation
4. **Fill constat amiable** (paper or E-Constat app)
5. **Get other driver's info:**
    - Name, phone
    - Insurance company & policy number
    - Vehicle registration
6. **Note witnesses** (names, contacts)

### Declaration Timeline

- **Standard:** Within 5 working days
- **Theft:** Within 48 hours
- **Immediate:** If severe injuries

### Declaration Methods

1. **In-person:** Visit agency with documents
2. **Online:** Via insurer's app/website
3. **E-Constat FTUSA:** Digital submission
4. **Phone:** Call assistance number on vignette

### Claim Processing

1. Declaration received
2. Expert assigned (within days)
3. Vehicle inspection at expertise center
4. Damage assessment
5. Repair authorization
6. Choice: Insurer's network OR own garage
7. Repair completion
8. Payment/reimbursement
