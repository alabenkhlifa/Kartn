## ‚ö†Ô∏è The Core Problem

**Tunisians cannot freely hold or use foreign currency.**

The Tunisian Dinar (TND) is a **non-convertible currency** protected by strict exchange controls (Loi n¬∞76-1976). This
creates a major challenge for FCR families wanting to buy a car in Europe.

---

## üÜï 2026 Game Changer: Foreign Currency Accounts

**Article 81 of Loi de Finances 2026** allows residents to open foreign currency accounts!

| Feature         | Details                                                     |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Who             | Tunisian residents (individuals only, not companies)        |
| BCT approval    | **Not required**                                            |
| Sources allowed | Allocation touristique, transfers from other forex accounts |
| Uses            | Payments abroad, online purchases, travel                   |
| Tax on interest | **0.01%** (nearly zero)                                     |
| Overdraft       | **Not allowed** - can only spend what's deposited           |

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Status:** Law passed, awaiting BCT implementation texts

## üìã Official Payment Methods for FCR

### Method 1: Allocation Touristique (Tourism Allowance)

Every Tunisian resident can convert TND to foreign currency annually:

| Person      | Annual Amount             |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| Adult (18+) | **6,000 TND** (~‚Ç¨1,760)   |
| Child (10+) | **6,000 TND**             |
| Child (<10) | **3,000 TND**             |
| **Couple**  | **~12,000 TND** (~‚Ç¨3,520) |

**How it works:**

1. Go to your bank with valid passport
2. Request allocation touristique
3. Receive via: cash, cheque, or **Carte Safar** (tourist card)
4. Valid for 60 days, single trip
5. Unused amount must be returned within 15 days

**Limitation:** ~‚Ç¨3,500 for a couple is far below most car prices!

---

### Method 2: Don TRE (Gift from Tunisian Abroad)

This is the **primary method** for FCR financing:

| Feature      | Details                                         |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Who gives    | Tunisian Residing Abroad (TRE) - family, friend |
| Who receives | Tunisian resident (FCR beneficiary)             |
| Amount       | Unlimited (for FCR purpose)                     |
| Process      | TRE pays seller in EUR, resident repays in TND  |

**Legal basis:** Explicitly mentioned in Loi de Finances 2026, Article 55

**How it works in practice:**

```
1. Family in Tunisia finds a car in Europe (‚Ç¨15,000)
2. Uncle in France agrees to help
3. Uncle pays the German seller ‚Ç¨15,000
4. Family gives Uncle's family in Tunisia ~51,000 TND
5. Car is imported under FCR, registered to family
```

---

### Method 3: BCT Authorization

If allocation + don are insufficient:

| Feature  | Details                             |
|----------|-------------------------------------|
| Who      | Tunisian residents                  |
| Process  | Apply to Banque Centrale de Tunisie |
| Purpose  | Complete FCR vehicle financing      |
| Timeline | Case-by-case, can take weeks        |

> ‚ö†Ô∏è Rarely used - most families rely on TRE network
