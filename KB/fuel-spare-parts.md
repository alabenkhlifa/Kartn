### Key Fuel Cost Insights

- **Government Subsidized**: Tunisia subsidizes fuel - the real cost is ~3.029 TND/L for gasoline and ~2.634 TND/L for
  diesel (2025 budget estimates)
- **Regional Comparison**: Tunisian drivers pay MORE than Libya & Algeria, but LESS than European countries
- **World Ranking**: Tunisia ranks 7th in Africa and 30th globally for cheapest fuel prices
- **Fuel Quality Concern**: Many Tunisian drivers believe domestically refined diesel may contaminate injection systems,
  leading to more frequent part replacements

### Fuel Cost Considerations for Car Import

- **Diesel vs Petrol**: Diesel is ~14% cheaper per liter
- **Fuel Efficiency Matters**: Given fuel costs, recommend fuel-efficient cars (5-7L/100km)
- **Hybrid Consideration**: While hybrids are available (Toyota, Kia, Hyundai, BMW, Mercedes), the market is still
  undeveloped
- **Electric Vehicles**: Market nascent; only 5 brands offer EVs; limited charging infrastructure (TotalEnergies leading
  EV station deployment)

### Parts Sourcing Options

1. **OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)**
    - Best quality, highest price
    - Available through authorized dealers
    - Guaranteed fitment

2. **Aftermarket (Ã‰quivalent)**
    - Good quality alternatives
    - MISFAT, LTM, Lucas brands
    - 30-50% cheaper than OEM

3. **Used Parts (Occasion)**
    - Tunisiecasse.com - Network of 20 junkyards
    - Up to 80% savings
    - 15-day minimum warranty

4. **Generic Parts**
    - Last resort for rare models
    - Quality varies significantly
    - Common for Chinese car owners

- **260+ automotive component companies** operate in Tunisia
- **90,000 direct jobs** in automotive sector (2022)
- **65% of parts manufacturers** are export-focused
- **2 million passenger cars** in circulation
- **Motorization rate**: Only 187 vehicles per 1,000 inhabitants

## 6. USEFUL RESOURCES FOR PARTS

### Online Marketplaces

- **automobile.tn** - Primary car marketplace, sellers usually have common models
- **AutoBeeb.com** - Buy/sell cars and spare parts
- **OpenSooq.com/tn** - Vehicle spare parts section
- **Tunisiecasse.com** - Used parts from scrapyards

### Physical Locations

- **Souk El Wardia (Tunis)** - Traditional auto parts market
- **Zone Industrielle Ben Arous** - Multiple parts suppliers
- **AD Tunisie network** - 400+ points of sale nationwide

### Tips for FCR Importers

1. Before importing, search automobile.tn for similar models to gauge market presence
2. Check if the car model is sold by Tunisian dealers (means parts infrastructure exists)
3. Contact local mechanics about parts availability for specific models
4. Join Facebook groups (Tunisian car enthusiasts) for real-world advice
